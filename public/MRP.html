My top 3 movies of all time

Avengers

The movie has a very good balance of heroism and humour, great characters' development, an interesting villain, great action-packed sequences

Spider-Man-No way home

A bigger, bolder Spider-Man sequel, No Way Home expands the franchise's scope and stakes without losing sight of its humor and heart

The Godfather

Francis Ford Coppola's crime saga follows the Corleone family in "The Godfather," "The Godfather, Part II" and "The Godfather, Part III."
